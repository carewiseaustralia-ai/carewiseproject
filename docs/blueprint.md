# **App Name**: CareWise Companion

## Core Features:

- Service Booking: Allows users to book services such as domestic assistance, shopping assistance, and companionship via a form.
- Service Catalog: Display available services, including domestic assistance, shopping assistance, transport, companionship, and welfare checks, with detailed descriptions.
- Contact Form: A form allowing potential clients to send inquiries and requests for pricing information.
- FAQ Generation (AI): Use a tool to generate a Frequently Asked Questions list, predicting common questions based on website content to help address user queries.
- Operating Hours Display: Display CareWise Australiaâ€™s operating hours (8 am to 4 pm, Monday to Friday) prominently.
- Team Introduction: Introduce the CareWise Australia team, highlighting their training and certifications (e.g., First Aid and CPR).

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A calming light blue (#ADD8E6) to evoke trust and compassion.
- Background color: Off-white (#F8F8FF) to provide a clean, accessible backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft green (#90EE90) for calls to action and to symbolize growth and well-being.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, which is suitable for headlines or body text.
- Use simple, universally understandable icons to represent each service offered.
- Maintain a clean, spacious layout with ample whitespace for easy readability and accessibility.
- Subtle, gentle animations on page transitions and interactive elements to provide a smooth user experience.
